Budgie

Thinking about money can be stressful. Keeping track of your money can be even more stressful. There are tons of budgeting apps available, but they can be confusing and intimidating. Budgie is designed for users who want to manage their money in a low stress, friendly environment!

OVERVIEW

WHY

Listing bills and income, debt, saving goals, budget and trasnactions.

WHAT

Anyone interested in understanding what they are doing with their money.

WHEN

When they are trying to save money, want to keep track of their money, or trying to get out of debt as soon as they can.

WHERE

Mostly at home or when out spending money.

WHY

COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

The problem

  • Apps on the market are difficult to understand and navigate

  • Only tracks money instead of helping teach users to manage their money

  • Many apps try to sell users credit cards

  • The overall appearance is dry and boring

Our solution

  • Easy to understand functionality

  • Focuses on teaching users how to manage their money

  • Fun motivatinal appearance

  • Easily add or delete items

  • Track and organize users money and bills
